# 英语二

## 2021-邀请参加会议

**Directions: Suppose you are organizing an online meeting.** 

**Write an email to Jack, an international student, to** 

1) **invite him to participate, and** 
2) **tell him about the details.** 

**You should write about 100 words on the ANSWER SHEET. Do not use your own name. Use "Li Ming" instead. (10 points)**

>Subject: Invitation to Participate in Online Meeting
>
>Dear Jack,
>
>I hope this message finds you well. I am writing to extend a warm invitation for you to join us at an upcoming online meeting we are organizing. As an international student, your insights and perspectives would be invaluable to our discussion.
>
>The meeting will take place on [insert date and time] via Zoom. The main purpose of the gathering is to discuss ways to enhance the academic and social experiences of international students at our university. We believe that your participation can contribute significantly to this goal.
>
>Please find attached the agenda and Zoom link for the meeting. If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to reach out to me. We look forward to your participation and hope to see you there.
>
>Best regards,
>Li Ming

>主题：邀请参加线上会议
>
>亲爱的杰克，
>
>希望这封邮件在你一切都好时到达。我写信是想热情地邀请你参加我们即将组织的一次线上会议。作为一名国际学生，你的见解和观点对我们讨论将非常宝贵。
>
>会议将于【插入日期和时间】通过Zoom平台举行。此次集会的主要目的是探讨如何提升我们大学国际学生的学术和社会体验。我们认为，你的参与将对实现这一目标做出重大贡献。
>
>请查收附件中的会议议程和Zoom链接。如果你有任何疑问或顾虑，请随时与我联系。我们期待着你的参与，希望能在线上会议中见到你。
>
>最好的祝愿，
>李明
>
>重点词汇：
>1. 延伸（extend）：表示邀请的正式表达。
>2. 国际学生（international student）：指在国外学习的学生。
>3. 见解（insights）：指深入理解或洞察。
>4. 贡献（contribute）：表示某人或某事对某一目标或结果有帮助。
>5. 议程（agenda）：指会议计划或要讨论的事项列表。
>6. Zoom链接（Zoom link）：加入Zoom会议的网络地址。
>7. 疑问（questions）：指需要解答的问题。
>8. 顾虑（concerns）：指担忧或疑虑。
>9. 参与（participation）：指参加会议、活动等的行为。
>10. 见面（see）：此处指在视频会议中看到对方。
>

## 2022 -介绍节日与邀请参加

#### Directions: Suppose you are planning a campus food festival. Write an email to the international students in your university to 

#### 1) introduce the food festival, and 

#### 2) invite them to participate. 

#### You should write about 100 words on the ANSWER SHEET. Do not use your own name. Use "Li Ming" instead. (10 points)

booth  cuisines 

>Dear international students,
>	I hope this email finds you well. I am writing to introduce to you our upcoming campus food festival, which is scheduled to take place next month. This event is an opportunity for us to showcase the diverse and delicious cuisines from around the world.
>	We would like to invite you to participate in this event by showcasing your traditional food and sharing your culture with our students. You can set up a booth and cook your traditional dishes, or simply bring them to share with everyone.
>	This is a great chance for you to promote your culture and make new friends. We are looking forward to your participation and hope to see you there.
>
>Best regards,
>Li Ming

>亲爱的国际学生们，
>希望这封邮件能让你们安好。我写这封邮件是为了向你们介绍我们即将在下个月举行的校园美食节。这个活动是我们展示来自世界各地的多元和美味佳肴的一次机会。
>我们诚挚地邀请你们参与这次活动，通过展示你们的传统美食，与我们的学生们分享你们的文化。你们可以设立一个摊位烹饪你们的传统菜肴，或者直接带来与大家分享。
>这是一个宣传你们文化、结交新朋友的好机会。我们期待你们的参与，希望在那里见到你们。
>此致敬礼，
>李明
>
>1. **diverse** (adj.) - 意味着种类繁多，不同。在这个上下文中，指的是来自世界各地的不同美食。
>2. **cuisines** (n.) - 特指不同地区或国家的烹饪风格和食物类型。
>3. **scheduled** (adj.) - 表示计划好或安排好的。这里提到的是校园美食节的时间安排。
>4. **opportunity** (n.) - 机会，指的是可以做某事的时机或情境。
>5. **showcase** (v.) - 展示或展出。在此邮件中，意味着展示传统食物和文化。
>6. **traditional** (adj.) - 传统的，指的是世代相传的习俗、做法或食品。
>7. **booth** (n.) - 小屋，摊位。在这里指的是活动现场设立的供参与者展示食物的小型空间。
>8. **promote** (v.) - 推广或宣传。邮件中提到的推广文化，意为通过分享食物来传播和介绍各自的文化。
>9. **participation** (n.) - 参与，指的是个人或团体在活动中的加入和贡献。
>10. **looking forward to** (phrase) - 期待，表示对未来的某事充满期待。

## 2023-做出建议和列举原因

#### Directions: An art exhibition and a robot show are to be held on Sunday, and your friend David asks you which one he should go to. Write him an email to 

#### 1) make a suggestion, and 2) give your reason(s). 

#### Write your answer in about 100 words on the ANSWER SHEET. 

#### Do not use your own name in your email. 

#### Use “Li Ming" instead. (10 points)

>Subject: Recommendation for Sunday's Event
>
>Dear David,
>	I hope this email finds you well. I understand you're trying to decide between attending the art exhibition and the robot show this Sunday. While both events sound intriguing, I would recommend you go to the art exhibition.
>	The art exhibition will not only allow you to appreciate various forms of art but also provide you with a deeper understanding of different cultures and perspectives. It can be a refreshing and inspiring experience. Plus, it's a great opportunity to meet new people who share similar interests.
>	I believe this would be a more enriching experience compared to the robot show. However, the final decision is entirely up to you. Enjoy your Sunday!
>
>Best regards,
>Li Ming

>主题：关于周日活动的推荐
>
>亲爱的David，
>
>希望这封邮件能在你安好之时送达。我了解到你正在犹豫周日应该参加艺术展览还是机器人展。虽然两个活动听起来都很吸引人，但我建议你去参加艺术展览。
>
>艺术展览不仅能让你欣赏到各种艺术形式，还能让你深入了解不同的文化和视角。这将是一次令人耳目一新且富有启发性的体验。此外，这也是结识志同道合新朋友的大好机会。
>
>我认为相比机器人展，这将是一次更加丰富充实的经历。不过，最终的选择完全取决于你。祝你周日愉快！
>
>此致， 李明
